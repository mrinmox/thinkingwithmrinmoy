The klara and the sun

The story is narrated by Klara, an Artificial Friend (AF), designed to serve as a companion for children in a future society deeply shaped by automation and social stratification. Klara is an observant, solar-powered robot with an extraordinary ability to perceive and interpret human emotions. Her perspective, both insightful and naive, offers a lens through which the complexities of human interactions and ethical choices are explored. Klara is purchased by Josie, a sickly teenager living in a world where children undergo a risky procedure called "lifting" to enhance their intelligence. As Klara becomes part of Josie’s life, she learns about Josie’s family dynamics, her deep bond with her childhood friend Rick, and the sacrifices parents are willing to make for their children’s future.
